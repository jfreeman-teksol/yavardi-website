Private Legal Practice

Partner Overpayment During Billing System Transition

A private litigation firm continued invoicing and partner compensation during a live billing and accounting system transition. Financial reports used to calculate partner compensation contained incorrect data. Compensation was distributed before errors were identified, resulting in partner overpayment. Correction required retroactive adjustment, delayed invoicing, and manual reconstruction of financial records across systems.

Distributed Legal Services

Client Reliance on Incorrect Institutional Guidance

A distributed legal services organization issued institutional guidance during active client service. Guidance was relied upon by staff and clients before inaccuracies were identified. Corrections occurred only after guidance had been acted on, requiring follow-up clarification, internal review, and remediation of downstream decisions based on the incorrect guidance.
